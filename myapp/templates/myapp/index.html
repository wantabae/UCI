UCI

Unified Carbon Interface

The Unified Carbon Interface (UCI) Sustainable Textile Certification establishes a globally recognized standard to validate the sustainability of textiles across the supply chain. This certification ensures compliance with environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles, preventing greenwashing and enabling brands to make verifiable sustainability claims. This document outlines the criteria, audit matrices, and acceptable thresholds necessary for a textile product or manufacturer to achieve UCI certification.
